# Bookmarks

Bookmarks provide a quick way to save important conversations for fast access. Unlike Collections, which are for organization, Bookmarks are for **quick access** to your most important conversations.

---

## Adding a Bookmark

### From Any Conversation
1. Open any conversation
2. Tap the **bookmark icon** in the top bar
3. Conversation is now bookmarked

### Visual Indicator
Bookmarked conversations show a filled bookmark icon in your library.

---

## Viewing Bookmarks

1. Navigate to **Bookmarks** in the bottom navigation
2. See all your bookmarked conversations
3. Sorted by when you bookmarked them (newest first)

---

## Removing a Bookmark

### From Conversation View
1. Open the bookmarked conversation
2. Tap the bookmark icon again
3. Removed from bookmarks

### From Bookmarks Page
1. Go to **Bookmarks**
2. Tap **Clear** to remove all
3. Confirm deletion

---

## Bookmarks vs Collections

| Feature | Bookmarks | Collections |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Quick access | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Organization | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Multiple per conversation | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
|排序 | By bookmark time | By name/date |

---

## Best Practices

### Use Bookmarks For:
- Conversations you reference daily
- Important project documents
- Learning resources you revisit

### Use Collections For:
- Grouping related content
- Thematic organization
- Team/project categorization

---

## Syncing

Bookmarks sync across your devices when you're signed in and connected to the internet.
