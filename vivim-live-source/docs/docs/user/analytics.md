# Analytics

The Analytics page shows you how you use VIVIM and how well the recommendation system works. This helps you understand your usage patterns.

---

## Accessing Analytics

Navigate to **Analytics** at `/analytics`

---

## Overview Metrics

### Total Feeds
Number of times you've loaded the For You feed

### Impressions
Total recommendations shown to you

### Clicks
How many recommendations you've tapped

### Dismissals
How many recommendations you've dismissed

### Click-Through Rate (CTR)
Percentage of impressions that became clicks:
```
CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) Ã— 100
```

---

## Activity Feed

See recent activity:
- When feeds were generated
- What was recommended
- What you clicked/dismissed

---

## Source Distribution

See where your conversations come from:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity
- Other providers

Shows count and percentage for each.

---

## Managing Analytics

### Clear Data
To clear all analytics:
1. Tap **Clear** button
2. Confirm deletion
3. All stats reset to zero

### Refresh
Tap refresh to reload latest data from storage.

---

## Privacy

Analytics are:
- **Stored locally** - On your device only
- **Not shared** - VIVIM doesn't collect
- **Optional** - Can be cleared anytime
