## PART I: INCORPORATION

### Article 1: Name and Form

1.1 The name of the organization shall be **VIVIM DAO** (the "Organization").

1.2 The Organization is formed as a **Decentralized Autonomous Organization** under the laws of [Malta/Switzerland], utilizing smart contract governance for decision-making while maintaining legal compliance.

1.3 The Organization may operate under aliases including:
- "VIVIM"
- "VIVIM Platform"
- "VIVIM DAO"

### Article 3: Duration

3.1 The Organization shall have perpetual duration unless dissolved pursuant to these Articles.

## PART II: OBJECTS AND POWERS

### Article 4: Objects

The Objects of the Organization are:

4.1 **Primary Object**: To build and operate infrastructure for user-sovereign AI knowledge management, enabling users to own, control, and monetize their AI interactions.

4.2 **Subsidiary Objects**:
a) Develop and maintain software protocols for AI conversation capture and knowledge extraction
b) Create and govern decentralized data storage systems
c) Facilitate peer-to-peer knowledge sharing networks
d) Promote privacy-preserving technologies
e) Advance open standards for AI data portability
f) Engage in commercial activities incidental to the above

### Article 5: Powers

5.1 The Organization shall have full powers to do all things necessary or expedient for the achievement of its Objects, including:

a) **Technical Powers**:
   - Develop, deploy, and maintain software protocols
   - Create and manage smart contracts
   - Operate distributed networks
   - Provide API and developer services

b) **Financial Powers**:
   - Receive and manage funds in any currency including cryptocurrencies
   - Make investments
   - Enter into commercial agreements
   - Distribute dividends and rewards

c) **Governance Powers**:
   - Establish governance structures
   - Create and enforce rules
   - Manage membership
   - Coordinate collective action

d) **Legal Powers**:
   - Acquire and dispose of property
   - Sue and be sued
   - Enter contracts
   - Engage agents and employees

## PART III: CAPITAL AND TOKEN STRUCTURE

### Article 6: Token Structure

6.1 The Organization shall issue the following token classes:

| Token Class | Symbol | Quantity | Purpose |
|-------------|--------|----------|---------|
| Governance Token | VGT | 100,000,000 | Voting and governance rights |
| Work Token | VWT | Unlimited | Bounty and contribution rewards |

6.2 **VGT (VIVIM Governance Token)**:
- Represents voting rights in the DAO
- Transferable subject to compliance
- No fractional voting
- Minimum holding for proposals: 100 VGT

6.3 **VWT (VIVIM Work Token)**:
- Non-transferable (earned through contributions)
- Converts to VGT upon milestone completion
- Used for reputation tracking

### Article 7: Initial Capitalization

7.1 The initial token distribution shall be:

| Category | Percentage | Tokens |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Community Treasury | 40% | 40,000,000 |
| Founding Team | 20% | 20,000,000 |
| Early Backers | 15% | 15,000,000 |
| User Dividends Pool | 15% | 15,000,000 |
| DAO Reserves | 10% | 10,000,000 |

7.2 The Community Treasury shall be controlled by DAO governance.

---

## PART IV: MEMBERSHIP

### Article 8: Members

8.1 **Definition**: A Member is any individual or entity holding VGT tokens at the time of determination.

8.2 **Acquisition of Membership**:
- Membership is acquired by holding VGT tokens
- Minimum holding for active membership: 1 VGT

8.3 **Rights of Members**:
- Right to vote on proposals
- Right to propose legislation
- Right to access certain organizational records
- Right to participate in community programs
- Right to receive dividends (when declared)

8.4 **Duties of Members**:
- Comply with these Articles and the DAO Charter
- Act in good faith
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Maintain security of tokens

8.5 **Termination of Membership**:
- Voluntary: By transferring all VGT
- Involuntary: By violation of membership terms (per DAO Charter)

### Article 9: Token Transfers

9.1 VGT may be transferred freely, subject to:
- Compliance with applicable securities laws
- Anti-money laundering verification for large transfers
- Compliance with sanctioned jurisdiction restrictions

9.2 The Organization may refuse transfers to:
- Prohibited persons under applicable law
- Competitors (at Governance Committee discretion)
- Addresses on blocklists

## PART V: GOVERNANCE

### Article 10: Governance Structure

10.1 The Organization shall be governed pursuant to the DAO Charter, which is incorporated herein by reference.

10.2 The governance bodies shall be:
- Token Holder Assembly (supreme authority)
- Governance Committee (administrative)
- Treasury Committee (financial)
- Technical Committee (technical)
- Community Moderation Council (community)

### Article 11: Board of Governors

11.1 **Composition**: The Governance Committee shall serve as the Board of Governors for legal purposes.

11.2 **Legal Representation**: The Board shall have authority to:
- Execute contracts on behalf of the Organization
- Represent the Organization in legal matters
- Engage service providers
- Open bank accounts

11.3 **Duties of Board**:
- Fiduciary duty to token holders
- Duty to maintain organizational records
- Duty to ensure regulatory compliance
- Duty to report financial status

## PART VI: FINANCIAL PROVISIONS

### Article 12: Accounts and Audit

12.1 The Organization shall maintain:
- Accurate financial records
- Token holder registry
- Proposal and voting records
- Meeting minutes (on-chain)

12.2 **Audits**:
- Quarterly financial reviews by Treasury Committee
- Annual external audit (when treasury exceeds â‚¬1,000,000)
- Public reporting of all transactions

### Article 13: Taxation

13.1 The Organization shall:
- Comply with tax reporting requirements
- File necessary returns in relevant jurisdictions
- Withhold taxes where required
- Provide tax documents to members

13.2 Members are responsible for their own tax obligations on dividends received.

### Article 14: Expenses

14.1 The Organization may pay reasonable expenses:
- Development and infrastructure costs
- Legal and compliance costs
- Member rewards and grants
- Operational expenses

14.2 Expense policies shall be established by the Governance Committee.

## PART VII: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### Article 15: Limited Liability

15.1 Members shall have no personal liability for Organization debts or obligations beyond their token holdings.

15.2 The Organization shall indemnify committee members and officers against:
- Third-party claims arising from their role
- Legal costs of defending claims
- Judgments entered against them in their official capacity

### Article 16: Exculpation

16.1 Committee members shall not be liable for:
- Actions taken in good faith
- Errors in judgment
- Losses not caused by gross negligence

---

## PART VIII: AMENDMENTS

### Article 17: Amendment Process

17.1 These Articles may be amended by:
- Constitutional Proposal per DAO Charter
- 66% approval with 30% quorum
- Compliance with jurisdiction requirements

17.2 Fundamental provisions (Articles 1, 2, 3, 6, 15) require 80% approval.

---

## PART IX: DISSOLUTION

### Article 18: Voluntary Dissolution

18.1 The Organization may be dissolved by:
- 75% approval of Token Holder Assembly
- Completion of wind-down process
- Distribution of assets per Article 19

### Article 19: Asset Distribution

19.1 Upon dissolution:
1. Pay all creditors
2. Settle employee/contractor obligations
3. Return unspent grants to contributors
4. Distribute remaining assets to token holders proportionally

19.2 No member shall receive more than their proportional share.

---

## PART X: REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

### Article 20: Regulatory Adaptation

20.1 The Organization shall:
- Monitor regulatory developments
- Adapt governance as required
- Maintain compliance officers
- File necessary disclosures

### Article 21: Sanctions Compliance

21.1 The Organization shall:
- Not transact with sanctioned persons
- Implement KYC/AML procedures
- Maintain compliance with EU, US, and international sanctions

## PART XI: INTERPRETATION

### Article 22: Definitions

"**DAO Charter**" means the constitutional document governing the Organization's decentralized governance.

"**Governance Token**" or "**VGT**" means the VIVIM Governance Token.

"**Member**" means a holder of VGT.

"**Organization**" means VIVIM DAO.

"**Proposal**" means a submission for Token Holder vote.

### Article 23: Severability

If any provision of these Articles is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in force.

### Article 24: Governing Law

These Articles shall be governed by the laws of [Malta/Switzerland].
