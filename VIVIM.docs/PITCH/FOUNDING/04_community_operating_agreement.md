## PREAMBLE

This Community Operating Agreement ("Agreement") establishes the framework for participation in the VIVIM community. It complements the DAO Charter and Articles of Digital Association by defining how community members contribute, earn recognition, and participate in governance.

---

## SECTION I: COMMUNITY MEMBERSHIP

### Article 1: Definition of Community Member

1.1 A Community Member ("Member") is any individual who:
- Participates in VIVIM community spaces (Discord, forums, GitHub)
- Uses the VIVIM platform
- Holds VGT or VWT tokens
- Has completed basic onboarding

1.2 **Membership Tiers:**

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Newcomer** | Joined < 30 days | Access to public channels, basic support |
| **Contributor** | Completed 1+ contribution | Voting rights, profile badge |
| **Builder** | 3+ accepted contributions | Bounty access, early features |
| **Advocate** | Reputation score > 1,000 | Governance proposal rights |
| **Guardian** | Reputation score > 10,000 | Committee eligibility |

### Article 2: Community Spaces

2.1 **Official Spaces:**
- Discord Server (primary discussion)
- Community Forum (structured discussions)
- GitHub (code and documentation)
- Wiki (knowledge base)

2.2 **Code of Conduct:**
- Respect all members
- No harassment or discrimination
- Constructive criticism only
- No spam or self-promotion
- Privacy respected

## SECTION II: CONTRIBUTION TRACKING

### Article 3: Contribution Types

3.1 The VIVIM ecosystem recognizes the following contribution types:

| Category | Examples | Recognition Points |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **Development** | Code, reviews, testing | 10-100 pts/task |
| **Documentation** | Wiki edits, guides | 5-50 pts/task |
| **Design** | UI/UX, graphics | 10-100 pts/task |
| **Community** | Moderation, support | 5-30 pts/task |
| **Research** | Analysis, feedback | 10-50 pts/task |
| **Security** | Bug reports, audits | 20-500 pts/report |

### Article 3: Reputation System

3.2 **Reputation Score Calculation:**

```
Reputation = Σ(Contribution Points × Tenure Multiplier × Quality Multiplier)

Where:
- Tenure Multiplier: 1.0 + (months_active × 0.05), max 2.0
- Quality Multiplier: 1.0 (default), up to 1.5 for exceptional work
```

3.3 **Reputation Benefits:**

| Score | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| 100+ | Propose minor improvements |
| 500+ | Priority support |
| 1,000+ | Governance proposal rights |
| 5,000+ | Committee nomination eligible |
| 10,000+ | Leadership recognition |

## SECTION III: WORK AND COMPENSATION

### Article 4: Bounty Program

4.1 **Bounty Categories:**

| Category | Description | Reward Range |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| **Critical** | Security vulnerabilities | 500-10,000 VGT |
| **Feature** | New functionality | 100-1,000 VGT |
| **Bug** | Bug fixes | 20-200 VGT |
| **Documentation** | Docs improvement | 10-100 VGT |
| **Translation** | Localization | 20-200 VGT |

4.2 **Bounty Process:**

```
1. Issue Posted → 2. Work Submitted → 3. Review → 4. Approval → 5. Payment
```

4.3 **Quality Standards:**
- Code must pass linting
- Tests required for features
- Documentation updates included
- Follows contribution guidelines

### Article 5: Milestone Contributions

5.1 Community members can contribute to VIVIM Milestones:

| Milestone | Community Tasks | Reward Pool |
|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| M1: Foundation | Testing, feedback | 5,000 VGT |
| M2: ACU | Documentation, tutorials | 8,000 VGT |
| M3: Compose | Feature testing | 10,000 VGT |
| M4: Context | Beta testing | 12,000 VGT |
| M5: Social | Community building | 15,000 VGT |

### Article 5: Grants Program

5.2 **Grant Types:**

| Type | Duration | Amount | Requirements |
|------|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Micro** | < 1 month | < 500 VGT | Single deliverable |
| **Standard** | 1-3 months | 500-5,000 VGT | Project plan |
| **Major** | 3-12 months | 5,000-50,000 VGT | Full proposal + team |

5.3 **Grant Application Process:**
1. Submit proposal in forum
2. Community feedback (7 days)
3. Governance vote (5 days)
4. Contract execution
5. Milestone-based release

## SECTION IV: GOVERNANCE PARTICIPATION

### Article 6: Community in Governance

6.1 **Participation Pathways:**

| Pathway | Requirement | Role |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Voting | 1 VGT | Vote on proposals |
| Proposal | 100 VGT | Submit proposals |
| Delegation | 1,000 VGT | Represent others |
| Committee | 5,000+ VGT | Governance role |

### Article 7: Community Moderation Council

7.1 **Composition:** 5 members elected by reputation-weighted vote

7.2 **Responsibilities:**
- Enforce community guidelines
- Resolve member disputes
- Recommend policy changes
- Report to Governance Committee

7.3 **Terms:**
- 6-month terms
- Maximum 2 consecutive terms
- Staggered elections

## SECTION VI: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### Article 10: Conflict Resolution

10.1 **Resolution Process:**

```
1. Direct Discussion (24 hours)
   ↓
2. Community Mediation (3 days)
   ↓
3. Moderation Council (5 days)
   ↓
4. Governance Committee (final)
```

10.2 **Appeal Process:**
- Appeals go to Governance Committee
- Must be submitted within 14 days
- Committee decision is final

---

## SECTION VII: COMPLIANCE AND SAFETY

### Article 11: KYC/AML Requirements

11.1 **Tier-Based Requirements:**

| Activity | KYC Required |
|----------|-------------|
| Basic participation | No |
| Bounty rewards < 500 VGT | No |
| Governance proposal | No |
| Dividends > €1,000 | Yes |
| Grants > 1,000 VGT | Yes |
| Committee service | Yes |

11.2 **Restricted Jurisdictions:**
- Members from sanctioned jurisdictions may have limited participation
- Legal compliance takes precedence

### Article 12: Security Requirements

12.1 **Wallet Security:**
- Members must secure their wallets
- No sharing of private keys
- Multi-sig recommended for large holdings

12.2 **Bug Bounty Rules:**
- No black-hat activities
- Responsible disclosure required
- Rewards only for first valid report

---

## SECTION VIII: AMENDMENTS

### Article 13: Agreement Changes

13.1 This Agreement may be amended by:
- Community proposal (1,000+ reputation or 100+ VGT)
- 14-day discussion
- 5-day vote
- 50% approval with 10% quorum

13.2 **Fundamental Provisions:**
- Section I (Membership) requires 66% approval
- Section IV (Governance) requires DAO Charter alignment

---

## SECTION IX: TERMINATION

### Article 14: Membership Termination

14.1 **Voluntary Withdrawal:**
- Member may leave at any time
- No refund of contributions
- Reputation lost

14.2 **Involuntary Removal:**
- Code of Conduct violations
- Fraud or theft
- Harassment or abuse

14.3 **Effect of Removal:**
- Loss of all privileges
- Potential token confiscation (for cause)
- No access to community spaces

## APPENDIX B: CODE OF CONDUCT

### Core Principles

1. **Respect**: Treat all members with dignity
2. **Inclusion**: Welcome diverse perspectives
3. **Integrity**: Be honest and transparent
4. **Excellence**: Strive for quality
5. **Collaboration**: Work together effectively

### Prohibited Behavior

- Harassment of any kind
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Spam or unauthorized advertising
- Sharing private information without consent
- Impersonating others
- Market manipulation
- Illegal activities

### Enforcement

- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: Temporary suspension (24-72 hours)
- Third offense: Permanent ban
- Severe offenses: Immediate permanent ban
