## SUMMARY

For a startup building VIVIM in the Mediterranean region:

- **Best Value**: Sicily offers the lowest costs (40–65% savings vs US)
- **Best English Environment**: Malta (English is official language)
- **Best Quality/Price**: Mallorca or mixed team
- **Optimal Strategy**: Hire 2–3 developers in Sicily/Malta, 1–2 in Mallorca for balance

The Mediterranean region offers **significant cost advantages** (50–65% lower than US) while maintaining **good quality talent**, particularly for remote/hybrid arrangements.
